# Naledi Feature Graphic Spec

## Google Play feature graphic
- Size: `1024 x 500`
- Focus: brand mood first, app utility second

## Recommended composition
- Left or center: `Naledi` wordmark or star-flower brand mark
- Right side: a cropped phone mockup showing the home screen
- Background: blush pink gradient with soft glowing shapes
- Accent motif: floral-star shapes inspired by the Naledi mark

## Suggested headline options
- `A little light for your rhythm`
- `Gentle cycle tracking, made personal`
- `Track your cycle with more softness`

## Suggested support line
- `Daily check-ins, soft insights, and a calmer way to stay in tune`

## Avoid
- Too much paragraph text
- Dark backgrounds
- Purple-heavy palettes
- Overly clinical visuals
- More than one phone mockup unless the layout stays clean

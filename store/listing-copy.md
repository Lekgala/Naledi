### Full description
Naledi is a warm, beautifully simple cycle tracking app designed to help you stay in tune with your body.

Track your cycle, log daily symptoms and moods, and get thoughtful guidance that feels gentle instead of overwhelming. Naledi brings together your period dates, cycle predictions, check-ins, and wellness patterns in one calm, feminine space.

With Naledi, you can:
- Track your cycle with an easy, private daily rhythm
- Log symptoms, mood, flow, and notes in seconds
- See your cycle day, phase, and upcoming period at a glance
- Notice recurring patterns in moods and symptoms
- Get soft reminders and supportive daily guidance
- Keep your information stored locally on your device

Naledi is designed to feel personal, comforting, and easy to come back to, whether you are just getting started with tracking or building a steadier routine over time.

Important:
Naledi is intended for personal wellness and cycle tracking only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
